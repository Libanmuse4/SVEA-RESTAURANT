Our Story

A Little Corner of
Scandinavia

Svea is one of Andersonville's most beloved neighborhood gems — a cozy, intimate spot that keeps the spirit of Chicago's old Swedish community alive. With Swedish-inspired décor, homemade food, and a warm, personal atmosphere, stepping inside feels like visiting a Swedish grandmother's kitchen.

For 15+ years, loyal regulars have been coming back for the nostalgia, the limpa bread, the Swedish pancakes, and the korv.
